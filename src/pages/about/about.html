SIKURITI (users) ----- version 1.0.0

This is a final year project by computer engineering students of University of Ghana. This mobile app works with a Raspberry Pi to provide mobile-based surveillance system for the home/office. It includes custom Google Maps for routing decision and fire hydrants markers for the Police and Fire Service departments. Mobile app users, should note that some functionalities of the app (e.g. View Pi Image) will not work without the Raspberry Pi system.
